# Sprint 3: RCI Analysis (0x2A)

Type: Risk, Compliance, and Impact Analysis  
Deliverables: RCI process notes spreadsheet, Cybersecurity recommendations document

## Objective

Conduct Risk, Compliance, and Impact analysis for the 0x2A organization. Identify operational risks, map them to compliance requirements, assess potential business impact, and produce actionable cybersecurity recommendations.

## Approach

1. Risk Identification. Cataloged organizational assets and threat scenarios.
2. Compliance Mapping. Aligned risks to relevant regulatory frameworks.
3. Impact Assessment. Evaluated likelihood by business consequence.
4. Recommendation Synthesis. Prioritized controls and mitigations.

## Deliverables

Process Notes Worksheet contains the risk identification matrix with likelihood and impact scoring. Cybersecurity Recommendations document presents a prioritized action plan for leadership review.

## Skills Demonstrated

`Risk Assessment` `Compliance Frameworks` `Business Impact Analysis` `Control Selection` `Executive Reporting`
