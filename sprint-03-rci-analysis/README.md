# Sprint 3 — RCI Analysis (0x2A)

**Type:** Risk, Compliance, and Impact Analysis  
**Deliverables:** RCI process notes spreadsheet · Cybersecurity recommendations document  

---

## Objective

Conduct Risk, Compliance, and Impact (RCI) analysis for the 0x2A organization. Identify operational risks, map them to compliance requirements, assess potential business impact, and produce actionable cybersecurity recommendations.

---

## Approach

1. **Risk Identification** — Cataloged organizational assets and threat scenarios
2. **Compliance Mapping** — Aligned risks to relevant regulatory frameworks
3. **Impact Assessment** — Evaluated likelihood × business consequence
4. **Recommendation Synthesis** — Prioritized controls and mitigations

---

## Deliverables

- **Process Notes Worksheet** — risk identification matrix with likelihood/impact scoring
- **Cybersecurity Recommendations** — prioritized action plan for leadership review

---

## Skills Demonstrated

`Risk Assessment` `Compliance Frameworks` `Business Impact Analysis` `Control Selection` `Executive Reporting`
